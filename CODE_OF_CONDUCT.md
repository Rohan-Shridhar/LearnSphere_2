# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

LearnSphere is a welcoming, inclusive community where learners, educators, and contributors can share knowledge and learn together.

We are committed to providing a safe environment for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

All community members are expected to:

- Treat others with respect and kindness.
- Welcome diverse perspectives and experiences.
- Be constructive and focus on ideas when giving feedback.
- Use inclusive language.
- Follow the rules and guidelines for the spaces they participate in.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, bullying, hate speech, or discrimination.
- Threats, intimidation, or abusive language.
- Sexual content or exploitation.
- Encouraging self-harm or harmful activities.
- Spreading misinformation intended to harm others.
- Disruptive behavior that interferes with other people’s learning.
- Sharing personal data (doxxing) or targeting individuals.

## Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it as soon as possible.

- Use the project’s issue tracker (for example, create a **Bug report** or **Feature request** if appropriate) and include relevant details.
- If you believe immediate action is needed, describe the situation clearly and include links, screenshots, or steps to reproduce.

Reports will be reviewed by maintainers.

## Enforcement

Maintainters may remove or restrict content and accounts that violate this Code of Conduct.

Possible actions include:

- Warning or requesting edits.
- Removing content.
- Temporary or permanent bans.
- Restricting access for repeated violations.

Enforcement decisions are made to protect community members and may be taken without prior notice.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all interactions within LearnSphere spaces, including:

- Issue tracker interactions
- Community discussions
- Educational content and contributions
- Any other spaces operated by the project maintainers

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is based on widely used community standards and adapted for the LearnSphere project.
